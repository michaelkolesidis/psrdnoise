psrdnoise: 3-D demos

Various example uses of the 3-D version of psrdnoise.

  • Plain 3-D noise on a sphere, with animation
  • Fractal sum with displacements, Flow Noise style
  • Yellow sun, or something
  • Red sun, with a different look
  • A sphere with liquid on it
  • A sphere with displacement and bump mapping, both using the analytical gradient of psrdnoise to recompute normals
  • A really busy sphere with no other purpose than to use noise a lot
  • Dark energy core, a nod to our mapping of gradients
  • A smoke plume, or at least our attempt at one.
